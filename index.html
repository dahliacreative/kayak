The Challenge In June 2017,10 absolute beginners will be taking on the river Thames in kayaks

Travelling 30 miles a day the group will be leaving Lechlade, Gloucestershire on 17 June and (hopefully!) arriving at Tower Bridge 5 days later on the 21 June. If paddling 150 miles wasn’t gruelling enough, there’s 45 locks to contend with en-route to a turbulent tide in London.

It’s all in aid of The British Heart Foundation in memory of a little hero called Finn. The target is to raise £10,000 to fund research into the UK’s biggest killer – Heart Disease (whilst looking after our hearts in the process!).
